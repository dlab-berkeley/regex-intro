To the Moon, North Korea? Or Does a Rocket Have a Darker Aim?
By DAVID E. SANGER and WILLIAM J. BROAD		10:05 ET
WASHINGTON — Kim Jong-un is headed to the moon. That, at least, is one
of the official North Korean explanations for the testing last week of a
rocket engine that, if as powerful as the North claims, would rival the
commercial rockets that Jeff Bezos and Elon Musk, of Amazon and Tesla, now
use in their aerospace companies to fire payloads into space.
